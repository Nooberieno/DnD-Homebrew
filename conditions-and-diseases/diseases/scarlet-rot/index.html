Scarlet Rot

Scarlet Rot is a magical disease that progressively decays any living matter.

Effects of Scarlet Rot

A creature affected by Scarlet Rot has disadvantage on Charisma checks and vulnerability to all damage. Additionally, an affected creature has the Rotting condition, even if their biology normally makes them immune to the condition.

Ending Scarlet Rot

Scarlet Rot can be ended by a Lessor Restoration. This does not end the Rotting condition affecting the creature. A Greater Restoration cures both the disease and the condition.
