_Level 5 Transmutation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: 30 feet<br>
**Components**: V, S, M (a small rubber ball)<br>
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute<br>
**Classes**: Bard, Sorcerer, Paladin<br>


> *You dick I needed those*

---


You target one creature within range that you can see, warping its skeleton into flexible rubber.
The target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save its speed is halved, it has disadvantage on Dexterity saving throws and Dexterity checks and whenever it takes Bludgeoning, Slashing or Piercing damage, it takes an additional 1d6 damage.

The target can repeat the saving throw at the ends of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

*At Higher Levels.* When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 6th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 5th
