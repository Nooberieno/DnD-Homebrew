_Requires speaking Orcish to cast_

_Level 6 Evocation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: 120 ft.<br>
**Components**: V, S, M (a small piece of flint)<br>
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute<br>
**Classes**: Artificer, Bard, Cartomancer, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard<br>




---

A fiery magic circle with a 5-foot radius appears centered on a point within range. When a creature enters the spells area for the first time on a turn or its starts its turn there, it is lit aflame, and it must make a Constitution saving throw. It takes 8d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

*At Higher Levels.* If you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 6th.
