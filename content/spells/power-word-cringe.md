_Level 3 Enchantment Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: 60 ft<br>
**Components**: V<br>
**Duration**: Instantaneous<br>
**Classes**: Bard, Wizard<br>


> *Who needs fireballs when you can roast their dignity*

---


You speak loaded with acute embarrassment, forcing a creature within range to cringe. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or suffer 6d6 psychic damage as it reels from discomfort. On a successful save, the target takes half damage.

If the saving throw is failed by 5 or more the target has disadvantage on Charisma until the end of the next turn.
