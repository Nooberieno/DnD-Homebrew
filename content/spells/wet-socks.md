_Level 1 Transmutation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: 30 ft<br>
**Components**: V, S, M(a small amount of water or water based liquid)<br>
**Duration**: Up to 1 minute<br>
**Classes**: Bard, Wizard, Sorcerer<br>
**Subclasses**: _Arcane Trickster_ Rogue



---

You target a creature within range, causing their footwear to fill with water as if they stepped in a deep puddle. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or have their movement speed halved and suffer from the discomfort of wet socks.

The creature affected by this spell can make another Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a successful save, the effect ends for it.

*At Higher Levels.* When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.
