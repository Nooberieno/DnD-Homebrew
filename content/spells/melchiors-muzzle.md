_Level 3 Transmutation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: 60 ft<br>
**Components**: V, S, M(a strip of flesh from a hostile creature, which the spell consumes)<br>
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute<br>
**Classes**: Bard, Druid, Wizard, Warlock<br>




---

You target a creature size Large or smaller that has a mouth or other orifices used for verbal communication. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or its mouth and/or other verbal orifices meld shut for the duration. While affected, the target cannot speak, cast spells with verbal components, or use its bite attack if it has one. Telepathic communication is unaffected.

The target can use its action to make a Strength (Athletics) check against your spell save DC to forcibly open its mouth, ending the effect on a success.
