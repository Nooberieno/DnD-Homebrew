_Level 2 Evocation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: 60 ft<br>
**Components**: V, S<br>
**Duration**: Instantaneous<br>
**Classes**: Wizard, Sorcerer<br>




---

Summons a crackling spear of lightning and hurls it at a single target. Make a ranged spell attack, upon hit deal 3d8 lightning damage

_At Higher Levels_: When cast using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.
