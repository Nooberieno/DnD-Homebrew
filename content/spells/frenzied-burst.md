_Level 3 Evocation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: 175 ft<br>
**Components**: V, S<br>
**Duration**: Instantaneous<br>
**Classes**: Wizard, Sorcerer, Warlock<br>




---

You release a concentrated burst of yellow flame from your eyes towards an enemy in range within your line of sight. Make a ranged spell attack. On a hit the target takes 5d10 fire damage. Additionally you can spend an action charging the burst. When charged the burst now deals 7d10 firedamage on a failed save and ignores resistance to fire damage, however you are knocked prone from the backlash
