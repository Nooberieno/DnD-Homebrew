_Level 1 Evocation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: Self<br>
**Components**: V, S<br>
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 2 rounds<br>
**Classes**: Wizard, Warlock, Sorcerer<br>


> _Incantation of Ansbach, Pureblood Knight. Produces a blood blade from the side of the hand, then lunges at enemies to cleave through them. Charge to increase the number of slashes. Markedly different from the finessed swordplay of the dynast, this is an aggressive last resort of an incantation that gave rise to Ansbach's fearsome reputation_

---


You summon a blood blade from the side of your hand. You can then choose to immediately attack or charge the blade. As part of casting the spell, you immediately gain one charge.

You can charge the blade further by concentrating on the spell for additional turns for up to 2 extra turns for 2 extra charges.  A charge is gained at the beginning of your turn.
When you attack with this spell all charges are consumed, for each charge you have, you can make 1 slash. make a melee spell attack for each slash. The first slash deals 1d6 Slashing damage, the second 1d8 Slashing damage and the third 1d10 Slashing damage.

If your concentration is broken you can use your reaction to immediately attack a creature with the amount of charges you have. Your charges are then consumed.

If the spell is fully charged the Target must make a Constitution saving throw or have 1 level of [Bleeding](@/conditions-and-diseases/conditions/bleeding.md) applied to it.

_At Higher Levels:_ When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, each slash deals its normal damage plus an additional damage die of the same type for each spell slot level above 1st. e.g., 2d6, 2d8, and 2d10 at 2nd level).
