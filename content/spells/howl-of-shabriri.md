_Level 4 Evocation Spell_<br>
**Casting Time**: 1 action<br>
**Range**: Self (30-foot radius)<br>
**Components**: V, S<br>
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute<br>
**Classes**: Warlock, Sorcerer, Wizard<br>




---

You unleash a maddening shriek that incites violent obsession.  Each creature of your choice within 30 feet of you that can hear you must make a Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a creature is overwhelmed by madness for the duration.

While affected in this way, a creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than you, and if it willingly moves, it must move closer to you if able. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success. A creature that succeeds on the initial saving throw is immune to this effect for 24 hours.

For the duration, your own power surges. You have advantage on attack rolls and when you deal damage with a spell or attack, you deal an additional 1d8 fire damage.  At the same time, attack rolls against you have advantage.
