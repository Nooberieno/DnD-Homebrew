_**Avoidance.**_  The Rune Drachton will take no damage from succeeding on spell save DC rolls and half damage on failures.

_**Pack Tactics.**_  The Rune Drachton has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the monster’s allies is within 5 ft of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

_**Carbon Detection.**_  The Rune Drachton is able to sense the breathing of any creature within 1 mile via smell

_**Keen Senses.**_  The Rune Drachton has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell

_**Runic Singaling.**_  The Rune Drachton can communicate with all other monsters of the same breed within 240 feet

_**Runic Strength.**_  The Rune Drachton can spend 5 ft of movement to escape a grapple

_**Diffuse Presage.**_  The Rune Drachton does not provoke attack of oportunity when not moving through difficult terrain or climbing


### Actions


_**Multiattack.**_  The Rune Drachton makes 3 attacks, 1 bite and 2 claw attacks

_**Bite.**_  Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 37 (6d10 + 4) Piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed one a DC 20 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. 

_**Claw.**_  Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft. one target, Hit: 19 (3d8 + 6) Slasing Damage
