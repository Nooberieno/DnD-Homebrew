_**No Guard.**_  The monster has an AC of 0 and cannot benefit from any bonus to its AC, in exchange its attack rolls always hit the target.

_**Galvanize.**_  Whenever the monster would deal bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage, it instead deals lightning damage.

_**Quark Drive.**_  When the monster takes Lightning damage, it becomes supercharged until the end of its next turn. While supercharged, its speed increases by 20 feet and it has advantage on Dexterity saving throws.


### Actions


_**Multiattack.**_  The Iron Thunder makes four Thunder Punch attacks

_**Thunder Punch.**_  _Melee Weapon attack:_ +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. _Hit:_ 6 (1d4 + 4) Bludgeoning damage and 2 (1d4) Thunder damage.

_**Discharge (Recharge 5-6).**_  The Iron Thunder charges up a electricity and unleashes it close by. Each creature must make a Dexterity Saving throw or take 2d12 lightning damage. Using this ability deals 6 points of lightning damage to the Iron Thunder as its internal circutry gets damaged
