_**Pack Tactics.**_  The Rune Drachton has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the Rune Drachton’s allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

_**Keen Senses.**_  The Rune Drachton has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

_**Runic Strength.**_  The Rune Drachton can spend 5 feet of movement to automatically escape a grapple.

_**Diffuse Presage.**_  The Rune Drachton does not provoke opportunity attacks when moving through a creature’s reach, unless it enters difficult terrain or climbs during the movement.

_**Avoidance.**_  The Rune Drachton will take no damage from succeeding on spell save DC rolls and half damage on failures.


### Actions


_**Multiattack.**_  The Rune Drachton makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

_**Bite.**_  Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d10 + 3) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

_**Claw.**_  Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.
