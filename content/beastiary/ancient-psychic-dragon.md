### General Lore
Psychic Dragons are the most cunning and enlightened of their kind, rarely seen in the material world. Most dwell within isolated pocket realities, peering into other planes with an insatiable thirst for knowledge.
Encounters with a Psychic Dragon end in either profound enlightenment or utter annihilation - never in between. Those who fail to appease the dragon's curiosity vanish without a trace, their remains scattered across the void.
They are often powerful spellcasters and may know spells that have been lost to time. Adventurers are often hired by wizards to retrieve these spells from a Psychic Dragons hoard, but this usually doesn't end well

### Campaign Specific Lore
Psychic Dragons are ancient dragons and thus are children of Arcanusax and Miss Coral. Most Psychic Dragons died in the war against Decho, the Fallen Inferno|Decho. The only known survivor as of now is Vellichor, who is currently located below the Library of Orion. There is a legend that anything you cannot find in the library, is known to Vellichor, although most people old enough to know of the legend, are far too young to know Vellichor is the name of a Dragon and not some kind of ancient tome.

***Legendary Resistance (3/Day).*** If the Psychic Dragon fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.  
***Warped Space.*** The Psychic Dragon subtly alters the fabric of reality around it. Difficult terrain within 60 feet of it does not impede its movement, and creatures of the dragon’s choice treat all terrain within this range as difficult terrain.  
***Mind Over Matter.*** The Psychic Dragon’s Intelligence modifier replaces its Strength and Dexterity modifiers when making ability checks and saving throws. Additionally, it can use its Intelligence modifier for attack and damage rolls instead of Strength or Dexterity.  

### Actions
***Multi-attack.*** The Psychic Dragon can use its Reality-Shattering Roar. It then makes three attacks: one with its Bite and two with its Claws.  
***Bite.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 3d10 + 8 piercing damage plus 4d8 force damage.  
***Claw.*** Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 3d8 + 8 slashing damage plus 2d8 force damage.  
***Spellcasting (Arcane).*** The Psychic Dragon is a 20th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 24, +16 to hit with spell attacks). The dragon can prepare spells from the wizard spell list, learning up to 20 spells of 6th level or lower over time. Additionally, it always knows the following spells:  At Will: Mage Hand (invisible), Mind Sliver, Detect Thoughts, Dispel Magic 3/Day Each: Synaptic Static, Telekinesis, Wall of Force, Legend Lore, True Seeing 1/Day Each: Psychic Scream, Time Stop, Wish (can only replicate spells of 8th level or lower)  
***Force Breath (Recharge 5-6).*** The Psychic Dragon exhales pure magical energy in a 90-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 24 Dexterity saving throw, taking 12d10 force damage on a failed save, or half as much on a success. Creatures that fail the save are also knocked prone and pushed 30 feet away.  

### Bonus Actions
***Fold Space.*** The Psychic Dragon teleports up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.  

### Reactions
***Psychic Rebound.*** When a creature targets the Psychic Dragon with an attack or harmful spell, the dragon can force the attacker to make a DC 22 Intelligence saving throw. On a failure, the attack or spell is redirected at a target of the dragon’s choice within 30 feet of the attacker.  

### Legendary Actions
***Telekinetic Slam.*** The Psychic Dragon chooses a creature or object within 60 feet and hurls it up to 30 feet in any direction. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 22 Strength saving throw or take 4d8 force damage and be knocked prone.  
***Mind Crush (Costs 2 Actions).*** The Psychic Dragon targets a creature within 60 feet that it can see. The target must make a DC 22 Intelligence saving throw, taking 6d10 psychic damage and being stunned until the end of its next turn on a failed save. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and isn’t stunned.  
***Spatial Collapse (Costs 3 Actions).*** The Psychic Dragon chooses a point within 120 feet. The fabric of space momentarily collapses in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that point. Each creature in the area must make a DC 24 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 8d10 force damage and is restrained as space itself warps around them. A restrained creature can make an Intelligence saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.
