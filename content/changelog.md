## Spells
### Tusk's Grimoire
#### Removed
- Removed Hymn of Strengthening
- Removed Hymn of Frenzy
- Removed Hymn of Slumber
#### Changed
- Hymn of Protection now requires a reaction to reduce damage
- Hymn of Protection now lasts for a maximum of 1 minute with concentration
- Hymn of Agony had its duration changed from Instantaneous to 1 Round, since the spells effect lasts for 1 round
- Hymn of Blindness requires a Wisdom saving throw, with the effect happening on failure
- Hymn of Blindness had its duration changed from 3 minutes to 1 Round
- Hymn of Blindness no longer gives the affected target advantage on saving throws against being Charmed or Frightened
- Hymn of Rage no longer has a static save DC, instead it now uses the casters spell save DC
- Hymn of Rage now requires concentration
- Hymn of Rage has no effect on creatures immune to the Charmed condition
- Hymn of Ferocity no longer has a static save DC, instead it now uses the casters spell save DC
- Hymn of Ferocity now requires concentration
- Hymn of Ferocity has no effect on creatures immune to the Charmed condition
- Hymn of Combustion deals 6d10 Fire damage instead of Fire damage equal to your spellcasting ability modifier
- Hymn of Combustion now requires a Constitution saving throw instead of a Dexterity saving throw
- Hymn of Combustion no longer deals half damage to creatures within 5 ft of the original target on a failed Dexterity saving throw.

###### Effect
A bunch of Talisman have had their effect changed to no longer use percentages or because their upgraded versions uses percentages
- Blade of Mercy  gives an additional damage die on the weapon attack after a critical hit, instead of 20% extra damage
- Lance Talisman  gives 1d4 additional damage of the weapons damage die, instead of 5% extra damage
- Crimson Amber Medallion  gives 1d10 max hp, instead of +5
- Crimson Amber Medallion  gives 2d10 max hp, instead of 10%
- Superior Crimson Amber Medallion  gives 3d10 max hp, instead of 20%
- True Crimson Amber Medallion  gives 4d10 max hp, instead of 30%
- Dragoncrest Greatshield  decreases damage from Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing by 1d4 instead of 10%
- Dragoncrest Greatshield Reforged  decreases damage from Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing by 2d4 instead of 13%
- Superior Dragoncrest Greatshield  decreases damage from Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing by 3d4 instead of 16%
- True Dragoncrest Greatshield  decreases damage from Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing by 4d4 instead of 20%
- Fire Scorpion Charm increases fire damage by 1d12 instead of 15% and increases taken Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing damage by 1d10 instead of 10%
- Glacier Scorpion Charm increases Cold damage by 1d12 instead of 15% and increases taken Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing damage by 1d10 instead of 10%
- Lightning Scorpion Charm increases Lightning damage by 1d12 instead of 15% and increases taken Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing damage by 1d10 instead of 10%
- Sacred Scorpion Charm increases Radiant damage by 1d12 instead of 15% and increases taken Bludgeoning, Slashing and Piercing damage by 1d10 instead of 10%
- Fatal Stinger Charm increases damage from Scorpion Charms to 2d12 instead of 25%
- Shattered Stone Talisman increases damage from unarmed strikes by 1d8 instead of 10%
- Assassin's Crimson Dagger lets you choose up to 3 damage dice and regain a number of hit points equal to their total value after landing a critical hit instead of regaining 25% of the damage dealt by the critical hit
- Aged One's Exultation increases damage of weapon attacks by 1d6, stacking up to 3 times for 3d6 additional damage, instead of giving 5% stacking 3 up to 3 times for 15% additional damage
- Kindred of Rot's Exultation increases damage of weapon attacks by 1d6, stacking up to 3 times for 3d6 additional damage, instead of giving 5% stacking 3 up to 3 times for 15% additional damage
- Lord of Blood's Exultation increases damage of weapon attacks by 1d6, stacking up to 3 times for 3d6 additional damage, instead of giving 5% stacking 3 up to 3 times for 15% additional damage

- Ritual Sword Talisman adds 2d6 damage of the weapons damage type to weapon attacks when at max hp, instead of adding +5 damage when at max hp
- Ritual Sword Reforged adds 2d8 damage of the weapons damage type to weapon attacks when at max hp, instead of adding 3% damage when at max hp
- Superior Ritual Sword adds 2d10 damage of the weapons damage type to weapon attacks when at max hp, instead of adding 7% damage when at max hp
- True Ritual Sword adds 2d12 damage of the weapons damage type to weapon attacks when at max hp, instead of adding 10% damage when at max hp
- Winged Sword Insignia adds increasing damage to successive attacks (1d4 → 1d6 → 1d8) instead of (3% → 5% → 8%)
- Rotten Winged Sword Insignia adds increasing damage to successive attacks (1d6 → 1d8 → 1d10) instead of (6% → 8% → 13%)
- Twinblade Talisman increases the second attack of 2 weapon fighting by 1d12 instead of 35%
- BoltDrake Talisman reduces Lightning and Thunder damage by 1d12, instead of 5

## Subclasses
### Jujutsu Sorcery

#### Way of the Curse Monk
- Regaining ki in combat has been reworked to be less broken.
- The cost of Divergent Fist has increased from 1 to 2 Ki points
- The cost of Black Flash has increased from 4 to 5 Ki points
- Cursed Energy Master no longer gives you 2 Ki points at the start of your turn

#### Heavenly Restricted Fighter
- Enhanced Physicality no longer provides pseudo-expertise to Perception and Stealth
- Enhanced Physicality no longer provides proficiency in the Stealth skill
- Enhanced Physicality no longer gives disadvantage on Arcana checks
- Enchanced Physicality no longer forces you to not have a bonus to Arcana checks
- Unyielding Body now grants 1d6 bludgeoning damage or 1d6 + your strength modifier when you are wielding no weapons and are not wearing armor, instead of 1d6 + strength modifier and 1d8 + strength when not wielding weapons and not wearing armor
- Perceptive Reflexes has been fully changed: It now allows you to detect invisible or hiding creatures within 10 ft of you
- Supreme Physicality has been fully reworked: It now allows you to drop to 1 hp instead of 0 when not outright killed, once per long rest
- Limitless Potential now just grants you a +2 bonus to either strength, dexterity or constitution, this bonus can go above 20, but not 22.
