Talisman Pouch

A Talisman Pouch is a rune-stitched leather satchel designed to stabilize and channel a single magical Talisman. While worn, you gain the benefits of one Talisman placed inside the pouch.

A creature can benefit from up to three Talisman pouches at a time. If more are worn, the wearer chooses which three are active; all others are suppressed.

The pouch does not require attunement. Some Talismans are powerful enough to require attunement independently.

Additionally Talisman Pouches can only make you benefit from one Talisman that increase/reduce damage by a percentage at a time. If you have multiple active, the highest percentage is used.
