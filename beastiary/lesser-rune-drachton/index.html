Languages. understands Druidic and Primordial, but cannot speak

Proficiency Bonus. +3

Pack Tactics. The Rune Drachton has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the Rune Drachton’s allies is within 5 ft. of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.

Keen Senses. The Rune Drachton has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.

Runic Strength. The Rune Drachton can spend 5 feet of movement to automatically escape a grapple.

Diffuse Presage. The Rune Drachton does not provoke opportunity attacks when moving through a creature’s reach, unless it enters difficult terrain or climbs during the movement.

Avoidance. The Rune Drachton will take no damage from succeeding on spell save DC rolls and half damage on failures.

Actions

Multiattack. The Rune Drachton makes two attacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d10 + 3) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.
