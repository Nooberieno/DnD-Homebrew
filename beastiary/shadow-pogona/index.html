Languages. Common, Draconic


Challenge. 6

Proficiency Bonus. +3

Shadow Tag. A creature in the Shadow Pogona’s melee reach cannot avoid provoking opportunity attacks. It cannot take the Disengage action, and any effect that would prevent opportunity attacks, such as the Disengage action, being unseen and the Mobile feat, do not stop the Shadow Pogona from making an opportunity attack against it.

Actions

Mutliattack. The Shadow Pogona makes 2 attacks, one with its bite and one with its claws

Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage.

Shadow Breath (Recharge 6). The Shadow Pogona exhales shadow energy in a 40-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 28 (8d6) psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
